# Customer Journey Map for LLM-Cache

## Aware Phase

### Trigger Event
- **Event**: Developer or researcher encounters performance issues with Large Language Models (LLMs) in their applications.
- **Metric**: 80% of developers experience performance issues with LLMs.

### Friction Points
- **Technical complexity**: Difficulty in implementing caching solutions for LLMs.
- **Limited knowledge**: Lack of understanding about caching best practices for LLMs.

### User Emotions
- **Frustration**: Frustration with slow performance and high latency.
- **Anxiety**: Anxiety about meeting project deadlines and delivering results.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Easy onboarding**: Simple and intuitive setup process for LLM-cache.
- **Technical support**: Access to expert support for caching and LLM-related issues.

### Metric per Phase
- **Awareness**: 20% increase in awareness about LLM-cache among developers and researchers.

### Trigger Event

- **Integration**: Difficulty in integrating LLM-cache with existing infrastructure.

### User Emotions
- **Hope**: Hope that LLM-cache can solve their performance issues.
- **Curiosity**: Curiosity about the features and benefits of LLM-cache.

### Opportunities to Delight
- **Cost-effective**: Clear and transparent pricing for LLM-cache.
- **Seamless integration**: Easy integration with existing infrastructure and tools.

### Metric per Phase
- **Consideration**: 30% increase in consideration of LLM-cache among developers and researchers.
