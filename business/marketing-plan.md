 # marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
1. Line: **Optimized Caching for Large Language Models (LLMs) - llm-cache**
   Alternatives:
   1. Accelerate Your LLM Performance with llm-cache
   2. llm-cache: The Caching Solution for AI-Powered Applications
   3. Boost Your LLM Efficiency with llm-cache

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. Persona: **John Doe, AI Researcher at TechGiant Inc.**
   - Daily Job: Develops and maintains large-scale AI applications, including LLMs.
   - Budget: $10,000 - $50,000 per year for caching solutions.

2. Persona: **Jane Smith, CTO of StartupX**
   - Daily Job: Oversees the development and deployment of AI-powered applications for her startup.
   - Budget: $5,000 - $10,000 per year for caching solutions.

3. Persona: **Robert Johnson, Lead Developer at AI-Consultancy**
   - Daily Job: Consults with clients on AI-related projects, including LLMs.
   - Budget: $50,000 - $100,000 per year for caching solutions.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Target AI-related groups, job titles, and interests. Estimated CAC: $200 - $300 per lead.
2. **AI-focused Conferences**: Sponsorships and booths at events like AI Expo, O'Reilly AI Conference, and NeurIPS. Estimated CAC: $5,000 - $10,000 per event.
3. **Content Marketing**: Blog posts, webinars, and guest articles on AI-focused websites. Estimated CAC: $1,000 - $2,000 per piece of content.

## Content Cadence
1. **Week 1**: Announce llm-cache on LinkedIn, AI-focused forums, and through email newsletters.
2. **Week 2**: Publish a blog post introducing llm-cache and its benefits.
3. **Week 3**: Host a webinar demonstrating how to implement llm-cache in various AI applications.
4. **Week 4**: Publish a case study showcasing the performance improvements achieved with llm-cache.
5. **Week 5 - 9**: Continue sharing content on LinkedIn, AI-focused forums, and through email newsletters.
6. **Week 10**: Launch a targeted LinkedIn ad campaign.

## Launch Milestones
1. **D-30**: Finalize llm-cache product, complete documentation, and prepare marketing materials.
2. **D-0**: Launch llm-cache, start content marketing, and initiate LinkedIn ad campaign.
3. **D+30**: Evaluate initial response, gather feedback, and make necessary adjustments.
4. **D+90**: Review success metrics (DAU, $MRR target), assess product performance, and plan for future improvements.

## Success Metrics
1. **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Aim for 500 DAUs by D+90.
2. **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Target $50,000 MRR by D+90.
