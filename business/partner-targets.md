## Partner Integration Roadmap for llm-cache

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API               | Free-Tier Limits                | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                   | Revenue-Share Potential |
|---------------------------|---------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **OpenAI API**            | $18/month free credits          | M                  | Enhances LLM performance by reducing response times for users | Yes                     |

### Rationale for Selection

1. **OpenAI API**: As a leading provider of LLMs, integrating with OpenAI will allow llm-cache to directly enhance performance for users leveraging OpenAI's models, addressing the pain point of latency.

2. **Hugging Face**: This platform hosts a variety of models and datasets. By integrating with Hugging Face, llm-cache can offer users a broader selection of models while optimizing their performance through caching.

3. **AWS Lambda**: This serverless computing service allows for scalable caching solutions without the need for server management, making it easier for users to implement llm-cache in their workflows.

4. **Google Cloud Functions**: Similar to AWS Lambda, this integration will enable users to deploy caching solutions in a serverless environment, enhancing performance without additional infrastructure overhead.

5. **Azure Functions**: Targeting enterprise users who rely on Microsoft services, this integration will facilitate seamless use of llm-cache within existing Azure ecosystems.

6. **Redis Labs**: As a popular caching solution, integrating Redis will provide users with a robust and fast in-memory store, significantly improving the performance of LLMs.

7. **Algolia**: This integration will enhance the search capabilities of LLM-generated content, providing users with faster retrieval times and improved user experiences.

8. **Zapier**: By automating workflows between llm-cache and other SaaS applications, users can streamline their processes, making it easier to integrate caching into their existing tools. 

### Conclusion

This roadmap outlines strategic partnerships that will enhance the functionality and marketability of llm-cache, addressing specific user needs while also providing potential revenue-sharing opportunities. Prioritizing integrations with affiliate programs will maximize revenue potential and user adoption.
